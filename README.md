# AGI Lab (GMND/AGI_Lab)

## Mission
To explore the frontiers of Artificial General Intelligence through rigorous scientific methodology, reproducible experiments, and systematic documentation.

## Core Research Pillars
1. **Cognitive Architectures**: Developing scalable models of reasoning and memory.
2. **Self-Evolving Systems**: Researching autonomous agents capable of improving their own code and logic.
3. **Neuro-Symbolic Integration**: Combining neural perception with symbolic reasoning.
4. **Alignment & Safety**: Ensuring AGI trajectories remain beneficial to humanity.

## Repository Structure
- `/research`: Theoretical papers, hypotheses, and literature reviews.
- `/experiments`: Code, datasets, and execution logs.
- `/logs`: Systematic tracking of all research progress.
- `/docs`: Documentation of methodologies and protocols.
